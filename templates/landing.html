Purpose

Attendance management in college lectures has long relied on methods that are easily manipulated. Paper sheets and static QR codes share a common weakness — they verify identity loosely. This practice, known as proxy attendance, undermines academic integrity.

The purpose of Advanced Attendance Management System (AAMS) is to address this problem directly by building a platform that makes proxy attendance technically difficult to execute — not just against policy, but against the system itself.

Core Objectives

Eliminate Proxy Attendance

The system silently runs a WebGL routine on the student's browser. The unique GPU hash is recorded; duplicate hashes from different accounts are rejected and flagged.

Rotating QR Codes

Signed tokens auto-rotate every N seconds. This adds a time-bound layer on top of the device-bound fingerprint layer, rendering screenshots useless.

Flexible Session Control

Teachers can switch between Live QR and Manual Marking modes effortlessly without resetting previous data for the same class.
